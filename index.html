About Me

I discovered my passion for Photography in my last year of college and purely due to an amazing teacher. He inspired me to want to take this passion further and completed a Design Degree majoring in Photography in 2007 from Massey University, Wellington. During this time I was introduced to many areas in Photography and with my other Passion being Sports, this was a great way to combine the two.

Photographing for Cricket Wellington for a number of seasons allowed me to get stuck into my passions and begin to be creative with what I was Photographing. Along with Sports I am fascinated with Stadium architecture and the development of these large structures over the many years. This research into stadium architecture in New Zealand began in my final year of my degree and I hope to one day publish my findings, purely through Photographic evidence.

I am now freelancing, Photographing for myself and others. This include events such as Weddings, Milestones and anything anyone requires.

And from the first Photographer to ever inspire me: "You see, I'm not interested in mediocrity in photography. I'm not interested in selling cat shit to dogs. I just want to do my own thing. If people like my work, all the better. If they don't, too bad." - Ralph Gibson
